University of Redlands

Home About Programs

The University of Redlands is a private university located in Redlands, California.

It is known for its strong programs in liberal arts, business, education, and geographic information systems.

Fun Facts

  • The University of Redlands was founded in 1907.
  • It is home to one of the top GIS graduate programs in the United States.
  • The campus is known for its historic architecture and orange groves.

GIS Programs at the University of Redlands

  • Master of Science in Geographic Information Systems (MS-GIS)
  • Certificate programs in GIS and spatial analysis
  • Hands-on training with ArcGIS and web GIS technologies
